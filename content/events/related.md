# Events related to Standards and Activties of the Modelica Association 

The following events are not organized by the Modelica Association. Their organziation however involves members of the Modelica Association and the events cover Modelica or FMI and/or any other standard of the Modelica Association. 

The events may not be tool neutral but they should be close to Modelica Association Standards.
