## New SSP Project Leader and Deputy of the SSP Project - Pierre Mai succeeds Jochen Köhler, and Robert Hällqvist is new deputy

The System Structure and Reparametrization (SSP) project members has elected Pierre Mai (PMSF IT Consulting Pierre R. Mai) as new project leader of the SSP project, and Robert Hällqvist (Saab) as his de deputy.
 
Pierre Mai is the founder and CEO of PMSF IT Consulting Pierre R. Mai and is involved in many different standardization organizations including ASAM e.V., Modelica Association, prostep ivip Association. He was one of the initiators of the SSP Standard and for many years already served as deputy project leader. He is one of the main developers of both the FMI and the SSP and layered standards within Modelica Association!
 
Robert Hällqvist has a PhD within the field of M&S credibility assessment, technical fellow at Saab Aeronautics. 
His involvement in the SSP Project: Industrial SSP end-user and contributor.

Pierre Mai follows Jochen Köhler (ZF Group), who has been been the project leader of the Modelica Association Project SSP from the beginning in 2014.
During this time, the SSP standard was developed from scratch, the version 1.0 and 2.0 have been release, and SSP has been adopted by many tool vendors and industrial users.
We want to thank him for his leadership and great contributions!
