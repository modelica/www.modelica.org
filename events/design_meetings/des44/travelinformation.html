Travel and accomodation information

Travel and accomodation information to the design meeting in Linköping, Sweden

The 44'th Modelica Design Meeting

Linköping 13-15/6 2005






Meeting Location

The design meeting is held at Linköping University, Sweden. The Department of Computer and Information Science (IDA) at Linköping University, which is the largest computer science department in northern Europe.

Travel information

How to get to Linköping:

Linköping airport is served by Skyways (partner of SAS). Norrköping airport by SAS from Copenhagen or Stockholm.
  • From Stockholm (200 km):
    • flight to Linköping (Skyways)
    • direct train to Linköping
    • Arlanda express (to Stockholm) 20 min + direct train to Linköping (between 1 hour 45 min and 2.5 hours)
    • car (ca 2 - 2.5 hours, take E4, southbound)
  • From Norrköping (40 km):
    • taxi
    • train to Linköping
    • car (ca 0.5 hours, take E4, southbound)
  • From Stockholm Skavsta airport, Nyköping (100km):
    • airport shuttle busses (flygbuss) to Linköping (ca 1.5 hours)
    • car (ca 1 - 1.5 hours, take E4, southbound)
  • Göteborg Landvetter airport (300km):
    • flight to Linköping (Skyways)
    • train (between 3.5 and 4.5 hours)
    • car (ca 3 hours, first to Jönköping, then E4 northbound)
  • From Malmö (450 km):
    • direct train to Linköping (ca 3 hours)
    • car
  • From Copenhagen Kastrup airport (ca 450km):
    • Direct train from airport to Malmö, then direct train to Linköping (3.5 hours in total)

How to get to the University

You can reach the University (Campus Valla) from downtown either by taxi or by bus.
  • Taxi
    Taxi Kurir 013-10 10 00
    Taxibil 013-14 60 00
  • Bus
    Take the bus 202 (towards Lambohov).

A map of Linköping with Campus Valla can be found here.

At the Campus

The meeting will take place at the Dept. for Computer and Information Science (Campus Valla), building B ("B-huset") highlighted on the map below. The best entrance to use is nr 27.


Another map of the university, with entrance numbers.

The primary meeting room is called "John von Neumann" and the secondary room is called "Donald Knuth". Their location at the department is shown on these maps: John von Neumann, Donald Knuth

Accomodation

12 rooms have been reserved at Scandic West (Ryd, Linköping) an can be booked for 880 SEK (approx. 95 EUR). They will be reserved until 2005-06-01, reservation code: "Modelica".
